Packages used

Apart from base R functionality, the patherit package uses a number of 3rd party R-packages:

  • For tree processing: ape v5.2 (Paradis et al. 2016);
  • For reporting: data.table v1.11.8 (Dowle and Srinivasan 2016);
  • For the POUMM and PP estimates: POUMM v2.1.0 (Mitov and Stadler 2017), boot v1.3.20 (Canty and Ripley 2016);
  • For testing: testthat v2.0.0 (Wickham 2016).
